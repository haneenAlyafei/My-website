How Do You Maintain Your Skin?

Oily Skin

  • Excess Oil Production
  • Shiny Appearance
  • Prone to Acne and Breakouts
  • Better Protection Against Wrinkles
  • Seasonal Changes

Dry Skin
  • Flakiness and Peeling
  • Rough Texture
  • Sensitivity and Redness
  • Fine Lines and Wrinkles
  • Imbalanced Oil Production

Combination Skin

  • Dry or Normal Cheeks
  • Larger Pores in the T-Zone
  • Dual Concerns
  • Need for a Custom Skincare Routine
  • Seasonal Changes

Normal Skin

  • Balanced Oil and Moisture
  • Invisible or Small Pores
  • Smooth Texture
  • No Major Skin Issues
  • Healthy and Radiant Appearance
